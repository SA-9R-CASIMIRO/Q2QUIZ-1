Philippine's Traditional Music Instrument's

Instrument Type of Instrument How is it used
Kudyapi Plucket String Play a constant drone with one string and play the melody with the other
Bungkaka Idiophone Strike the instrument against your hand to make a buzz sound
Gongs Idiophone Strike it with only 1 wooden beater
Gambal Idiophone Either played by a wooden beater or by your hand
Dabakan Percussion Pivot the 2 sticks bewtween thumb and forfinger using your wrist to strike the drumhead
